# Strategic Product Analytics Dashboard

## Overview
I developed an **executive-level Product Analytics Dashboard** that provides clear, actionable insights into revenue, profit, and trends. This dashboard enables management to make fast, informed decisions without sifting through raw data.

![image alt](https://github.com/Ilias-at/Strategic-Product-Analytics-Dashboard/blob/main/product-analytics-dashboard.png.png?raw=true)

---

## My Role & Contribution
I acted as a **Strategic Business & Data Analyst**, responsible for the **entire workflow** from understanding management needs to delivering actionable insights:

- Interpreted the **management request** and defined KPIs for a one-page executive dashboard.  
- Built and structured the **database** to unify product, sales, and discount data.  
- Wrote **SQL queries** to calculate revenue, profit, and other key metrics.  
- Designed the **Power BI dashboard** with interactive visuals for clear executive insights.  
- Provided **strategic recommendations** based on data analysis.

---

## Business Problem Solved
Management needed a **high-level view of product performance** to:

- Identify top-performing regions and products  
- Track revenue and profit trends  
- Understand the effect of discounts on profitability  

This dashboard transformed raw data into **actionable insights**, saving management time and improving strategic decision-making.

---

## Key Metrics & Features
- **Revenue & Profit** by region and over time  
- **Year-over-Year (YoY) growth** for units sold and profit  
- **Revenue breakdown** by discount categories  
- **Interactive dashboard features** for easy exploration by executives
